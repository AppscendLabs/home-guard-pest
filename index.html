About Us

Home Guard is a locally owned pest control service in Marion, Arkansas. We serve all surrounding cities/counties within Arkansas!

My name is Alden Deese, and I started Home Guard in February of 2023. After doing pest control work for friends and family for years, I decided to open my own business to bring a quality service at a fair price to everyone. For years, folks in our area have been limited to large scale businesses that value their bottom line more than the customer. It is our top priority to meet every customer's needs and to ensure a job well done. We're a small business trying to make a name for ourselves in a market dominated by the big guys, which is why you can trust that we will always put you first.
